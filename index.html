Good to know

Questions, answered.

What should I eat near me tonight?

Tell us what kind of night you are having — date night, family night, quick bite, comfort food — and you get one confident local pick plus exactly what to order. No endless scrolling through listings or reviews.

How does FoodInMyCity pick restaurants?

Tonight's Pick is based on what is open now, how far away it is, the current wait, the weather, your taste history, and what locals are saving right now. You get one strong recommendation instead of hundreds of results.

What is The Daily Table?

The Daily Table is twelve dishes — not restaurants — that define what your city is eating today. It refreshes every morning so there is always something new to crave.

How do local food creators influence recommendations?

Trusted local food editors share the dishes they actually love. Their picks and trails feed into recommendations so you get honest, neighborhood-level guidance rather than paid placements.

Does FoodInMyCity use AI?

We use smart technology behind the scenes to help you decide faster, but the experience stays human and food-first. You simply tell us your night and get a pick you can trust.
